Detailed Description

Triple integrator dynamics model, where the jerk is the control input.

State vector x = [x1 x2... v1 v2... a1 a2...] Control vector u = [j1 j2...] where xi, vi, ai, and ji are the position, velocity, acceration, and jerk in dimension i.
