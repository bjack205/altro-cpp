Detailed Description

Simple kinematic model of a unicycle / differential drive robot.

Has 3 states and 2 controls.

Mathematical Formulation

\[ \begin{bmatrix} \dot{x} \\ \dot{y} \dot{\theta} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} v \cos(\theta) \\ v \sin(\theta) \\ \omega \end{bmatrix} \]

where

\[ u = \begin{bmatrix} v \\ \omega \end{bmatrix} \]
