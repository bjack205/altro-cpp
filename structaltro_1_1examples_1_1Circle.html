Compute the squared distance function.

Mathematical Formulation

The squared distance function is defined to be:

\[ || p_x - c_x ||_2^2 - r^2 || \]

where \( p_x \) is the position of the robot, \( c_x \) is the center of the circle, and \( f \) is the radius of the circle.
